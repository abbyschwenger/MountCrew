ABOUT


Mount Saint Joseph Academy Crew is an elite high school rowing team offering competitve and fun rowing for young women.


We are a family, on and off the water, who have the mental and physical capacity of achieving our goals. We are dedicated to one another's and the team's successes. We are true sports women and Champions.


Find out more about Mount Saint Joseph Academy's tradition of excellence here.


Our boathouse is located at 801 Washington St, Conshohocken, PA 19428.
