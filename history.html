Our Founders


Among our founders is the dynamic husband-wife duo who we would be remiss not to recognize. Lois Trench-Hines and George Hines have been with Mount Crew from the beginning, providing support for the program both financially and personally.

Their dedication to rowing and especially to Mount Crew is unparalleled, and we are eternally thankful for the legacy they have left on the program.
