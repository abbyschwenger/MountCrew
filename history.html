Our Founders


Among our founders are two people who we would be remiss not to recognize. Lois Trench-Hine and George Hines have been with Mount crew from the beginning, providing support both financially and personally.

Their dedication to rowing and especially to Mount Crew is unparalleled, and we are eternally thankful for the legacy they have left on the program.
