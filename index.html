As we celebrate the 20th year of our program, we reflect upon our successes both on and off the water and the many people who have upheld our legacy throughout the years. Since our 1998-1999 season, our program has achieved an impressive

  • 7 finshes as top high school at Head of the Charles Regatta
  • 17 consectuive years as winners of the Brigantine Women's Sweep Points Trophy during PSRA racing season
  • 50 Philadelphia City Championships
  • 25 Stotesbury Cup Championships
  • 15 National titles
  • 1 USRowing Youth Nationals title
  • 1 Olympian
  • Over 200 athletes who have gone on to compete at the collegiate level
