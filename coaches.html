Coaches

Meg Kennedy



Long-time Head Coach, Meg leads Mount Crew fearlessly. She has been coaching at The Mount since 1999 and as such has led the program to numerous successes. She is a graduate of Drexel University, where she was a coxswain for their women's rowing team. Meg is a member of Fairmount Rowing Association, a founder of Whitemarsh Boat Club, serves on the Board of Directors of the Philadelphia Scholastic Rowing Association, and is a Mid-Atlantic Representative to the Youth Committee for USRowing. Meg is also the president of ELM Medical, based in Conshohocken.

Mike McKenna



McKenna, Mount Crew co-founder and varsity coach, is well-known in the Philadelphia area and beyond as a skilled leader in the sport of rowing. McKenna is a graduate of Drexel University, where he rowed for their men's team. He is a member of Fairmount Rowing Association and outside of crew enjoys spending time with his granddaughter Astrid and dog Rio.

Alanna McCloy



Alanna, a Mount Crew alum herself, has served as head Freshman/Novice coach since 2015. She previously coached the fours, leading them to several Youth National Championship qualifications and City Championship medals. Her alma mater is Villanova University, where she studied economics and prepared herself to work for Meg at ELM Medical!

Kiera McCloy



Kiera has been coaching the varsity fours since her debut coaching season in 2016. She is a Mount Crew alum herself, and she is a member of the only Mount Crew boat to ever win Youth National Championships. She continued to row through college and began coaching immediately after, never stepping away from the sport! Kiera is now a graduate student at Villanova University, where she also received her undergraduate degree, studying Business.

Kara Lederer



Kara, another Mount Crew alum, has been on the freshman/novice coaching team since 2014. She has had a tremendous impact on new rowers, leading her first ever boat to a silver medal at City Championships. Kara attended Penn State University, where she studied Communication Arts and Sciences. Outside of crew, Kara iw a Client Operations Specialist for Adelphi Research, a healthcare focused global market research consultancy, where she handles operations, compliance, and project manage studies for her clients.

Ellie Coles



Ellie began her coaching career at The Mount in 2017 with our varsity fours. She herself is not a Mount alum, but she was a coxswain for the men's team at The university of virginia where she won gold at Dad Vails with her lightweight 4+. Ellie works as a chemical engineer in Philadelphia and enjoys riding her bike.
