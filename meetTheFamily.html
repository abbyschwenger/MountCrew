Bigs & Littles: Celebrating a Sisterhood


This Year, our team has implemented a little sister/big sister program.

The bigs are upperclassmen, and the littles are novices who are just learning to row.

In a sport like rowing, where most of the athletes have not been exposed to it until high school, this program makes the freshmen feel welcome and have someone to turn to with questions about the still-unfamiliar sport.
